Home Alone

Jeter Arevalo



4.4/5

This movie is a classic, I believe everyone has watched this movie atleast
once because its very funny and a chill movie to watch with your friends
or family. This is a comedy movie telling the story of a kid that got left
home by his parents when they were in a rush to get to the airport. Now
obviously this won't actually happen but it's a comedy movie so anything
could happen. Later on in the movie with this kid happen to be home alone
some burgalers try to get into his house but he prevents that with very
silly traps. Overall this movie is very funny to
watch with friends and family and gets a 4.4/5 as a rating.
