Demon Slayer : Mugen Train

Jeter Arevalo




4.8.5

This is an amazing Anime movie with a lot of heart racing scenes! This movie is the continuation
of the amazing anime called Demon Slayer. This movie has amazing animation with the fight
scenes looking like a work of art. This movie tells the story of four demon slayers in a train
that has a very powerful demon that is trying to kill all the people on the train. While
they fight the demon they also show the backstory of the demon and one demon slayer that wasn't
properly introduced yet. Spoiler alert the demon is killed with the amazing soundtrack in
the background. The movie end with a death of one demon slayer that was fighting a
very powerful demon that came to help the other demon. The death scene was very
sad with the death of a really good demon slayer. With the amaing animation, soundtrack, and character developement I rate this movie a 4.8/5
