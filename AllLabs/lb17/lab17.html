Mental Health Awareness for everyone.

Many kids in highschool struggle a lot with mental health. Some teens in highschool don't even speakto anyone about the struggles they go through because of the uncomfortable enviroment they are placed in. We all should make a comfortable enviroment for anyone even adults because anyone can struggle with mental health. For example if a kid has depression it can be because of many things but we will never know because the student/child will never talk about it to anyone because of the envirment we create for the people that struggle.

Overall Mental health should be talked a lot and should bring light to it, to create a comfortable enviroment for everyone to be able to share without anyone judging or making unnessary comments.
